♦♦ My education ♦♦

My journey about my studies from primary school, secondary school and university

Sekolah Kebangsaan Pusat Juasseh

 My primary school is located in Kampung Kuala Juasseh, 72500 Juasseh, Negeri Sembilan and the distance from home to my school is only 3km. Then, I was a school superintendent and also I like to enter various types of competitions especially in sports. I stayed in the same primary school until I finished taking the UPSR exam

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Juasseh

 My highschool was located at Jalan Kuala Pilah Bahau 72500, Juasseh, Negeri Sembilan and the distance is the same as the distance from my primary school which is 3km. When I was in high school I held the position of assistant chief invigilator and I was very active in being the emcee for any event. Also, I took the literary and business streams when I was in grades four and five.

UiTM Campus Rembau

 Alhamdulillah, I got the opportunity to further my studies to diploma in taking Information Management course at UiTM Rembau. The distance of my university from my home is 37km and the location is located in Kampung Pilin 71300 Rembau Negeri Sembilan. I feel grateful because there are many wonderful experiences in my life in furthering my studies at UiTM Rembau.
